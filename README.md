# Polycarboxylate Ether(PCE) Superplasticizer | InnoNew Material
## Company & Product Overview
We are a professional China chemical manufacturer focusing on construction additive: **Polycarboxylate Ether(PCE) superplasticizer**, serving global concrete & dry mortar manufacturers especially Southeast Asia market.

We supply two mainstream PCE grades:
- Liquid PCE: 40%/50% solid content
- Powder PCE: ≥98% effective content

Our PCE superplasticizer owns high water reduction rate, low slump loss, excellent compatibility with various cement, widely used in ready-mixed concrete, precast component, grouting material and engineered mortar.
